# Tradeoff Analysis

Retry strategies involve multiple tradeoffs.

## Success Rate vs Risk

More retries:

- higher success rate
- higher risk of duplicate transactions

Fewer retries:

- lower success rate
- safer system behavior

---

## Automation vs Control

Automated retries:

- faster recovery
- less user effort

Manual retries:

- more control
- lower risk

---

## Compliance vs Growth

Aggressive retries may improve conversion but risk violating NACHA thresholds.

A balanced approach ensures sustainable system behavior.
